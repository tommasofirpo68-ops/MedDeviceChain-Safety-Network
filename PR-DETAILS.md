# Medical Device Safety Contracts Implementation\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces the core smart contracts for the MedDeviceChain-Safety-Network, implementing a comprehensive blockchain-based medical device tracking and recall system that prioritizes patient safety through transparent, immutable record-keeping and rapid response capabilities.\n\n## 🏥 Contracts Implemented\n\n### Device Manufacturing Registry Contract\n\n**Purpose**: Track medical device production batches, quality control tests, and FDA approval status with complete manufacturing transparency.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Batch Management**: Complete lifecycle tracking from production to deployment\n- **Quality Control Integration**: Immutable QC test records with authorized tester validation\n- **FDA Approval Tracking**: Real-time regulatory approval status and compliance monitoring\n- **Manufacturer Authorization**: Role-based access control for manufacturing entities\n- **Audit Trail**: Complete immutable history of all manufacturing processes\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-manufacturer`: Onboard authorized manufacturers with certification validation\n- `register-batch`: Create new device batches with comprehensive metadata\n- `add-qc-test`: Record quality control test results by authorized testers\n- `set-fda-approval`: Manage FDA approval status and regulatory compliance\n- `update-batch-status`: Track batch progress through manufacturing stages\n\n### Patient Device Tracking Contract\n\n**Purpose**: Link medical devices to patients while maintaining strict privacy protections for rapid recall notifications and emergency response.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Privacy-First Architecture**: Uses hashed patient identifiers to protect sensitive information\n- **Rapid Recall System**: Instant notification capabilities for device recalls and safety alerts\n- **Healthcare Authorization**: Role-based access for authorized facilities and physicians\n- **Emergency Access**: Controlled emergency access with comprehensive audit logging\n- **Device Deployment Tracking**: Complete device lifecycle from deployment to retirement\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `authorize-facility`: Register and validate healthcare facilities\n- `authorize-physician`: Onboard medical professionals with license verification\n- `deploy-device`: Track device deployment to healthcare facilities\n- `link-device-patient`: Create privacy-protected patient-device relationships\n- `trigger-recall-notification`: Initiate system-wide recall notifications\n- `emergency-access`: Provide controlled emergency access to patient data\n\n## 🔒 Security & Privacy Features\n\n### Data Protection\n- **Hashed Patient Identifiers**: All
